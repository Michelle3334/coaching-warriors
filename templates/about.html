About Coaching Warriors

How we coach

Our coaching methodology draws on adult stage development and complexity theory, adaptive leadership, appreciative inquiry and action learning. We use the Growth Edge Interview, the Immunity to Change process, Appreciative Inquiry, Gestalt and self embodied learning techniques and somatic work.

We work alongside our clients with compassion and curiosity to explore current thinking to see new choices and possibilities which can be discovered.

We explore with the client their core beliefs to find out whether they are still useful and work for them, or not.

About our concepts

Kegan’s concept of ‘subject – object’ is integral to our work. ‘Things which are ‘subject’ are the things which are held internally and so much a part of you that they are not seen or questioned. The things that are ‘object’ in our lives are the elements of our understanding which we can reflect on, look at and change our stance, (if we want to).

We help our clients to see the clues to discover the what one is ‘subject’ to, for example strong and negative responses which take up precious time and energy. Learning to observe these clues and responses and being curious about what they may mean, has reduced the negative impact and given more choices to our coaches and to our clients
